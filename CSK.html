About CSK

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are a franchise cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, which plays in the Indian PremierLeague (IPL)
plays its home matches at the M. A. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai. The team served a two-year suspension from the IPL starting July
2015 for the alleged involvement of their owners in the 2013 IPL betting case,[2] and won the title in its comeback season.
The team is captained by Mahendra Singh Dhoni and coached by Stephen Fleming.
